DoDD Suicide Prevention Policy

The DoD Directive (DoDD) 6490.14 is the first Department of Defense (DoD)-wide comprehensive policy on suicide prevention. It determines applicability, standardizes definitions, and assigns responsibilities for the Defense Suicide Prevention Program.
